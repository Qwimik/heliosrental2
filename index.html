Frequently asked questions

Is it necessary to declare real estate abroad?

According to the legislation of Ukraine, only officials, civil servants, as well as employees of health care, law enforcement, judges and prosecutors are obliged to declare real estate abroad. Other buyers may not show the availability of this property.

Is it necessary to declare real estate abroad?

To buy an apartment or house in the countries listed on our website, you do not need to obtain citizenship. A number of countries give an additional advantage — the issuance of a residence permit based on the purchase of real estate. If real estate abroad is bought for registration, the buyer needs to specify the terms of stay in the country.

Where is the best place to buy real estate abroad?

To select the appropriate country, you need to determine the purpose of this purchase. If the main task is to rent square meters, then you should pay attention to the list of investment-attractive countries. Buying real estate for relocation requires individual consideration of the issue. We are ready to consult within the list of countries indicated on our website.

How to buy real estate abroad?

The purchase of objects takes place according to the following algorithm: we determine the purpose of the purchase, choose the country and select the objects. Next: the trip to the country, the selection of the object and the purchase offer. If we are talking about apartments from developers, remote purchase is possible. If you want to buy real estate in Europe, your presence at the transaction is mandatory.

Foreign real estate in conditions of an unstable economy

The minimum return on real estate investments of 3-4% per annum is a rate that even a bank deposit does not give. At the same time, investing money in foreign real estate gives earnings of 6% per annum. Payments from the management company are guaranteed by the terms of the contract and do not depend on external factors - demand, tourist season, closed borders, etc.

The VRC agency is engaged in the sale of real estate in 22 countries of the world, and in each location there are investment-attractive objects that bring returns of 5% per annum.

The maximum profit is obtained when reselling square meters from the developer 1-2 years after purchase. Investing money in such a strategy brings from 30 to 60% per annum in currency.

Investing in real estate abroad is profitable, as the demand for rent has increased 6 times in the current year alone. This was also facilitated by the fact that more than 3.4 million people officially left Ukraine.

The war in the country contributed to the fact that it became easier for Ukrainians to buy real estate abroad. European countries introduced a regime of temporary asylum and allowed non-residents to obtain temporary status and at the same time gain access to the real estate market.

The foreign real estate agency guarantees legal security

The buyer of the object abroad is in a helpless state. In most European countries, it is impossible to buy real estate without a licensed intermediary. It is an agent who, at the level of local legislation, has the right to select options and accompany the agreement.

Catalogs, real estate marketplaces and classifieds sites are traps for gullible buyers. Among the offers, where the indicated cost seems advantageous, there are often problematic objects where you can invest money in a project with an incomplete package of documents.

The consequences of the wrong choice of the object may be the impossibility of later obtaining a residence permit, reselling the property abroad, or later you will discover operational deficiencies that must be eliminated at your own expense.

When contacting a foreign real estate agency, you insure yourself against surprises, because only verified objects of the secondary market enter our database. And developers undergo a separate examination by the company's legal department.

Although VIP Realty Company is located in Odesa, each of the countries where we work has its own offices and partners who act as a party accompanying the client at all stages of the transaction.

Investing in real estate abroad to obtain GNP or citizenship

Buying real estate abroad makes it possible to obtain legal status in the form of a residence permit, permanent residence or citizenship.

Property abroad for visa-free travel is available in Greece, Spain, Tenerife, Portugal, Poland and other countries. Legalization takes place through the issuance of a Golden Visa. If you buy an apartment in Greece for 250,000 euros through an agency, you can expect to receive citizenship.

Using the example of other countries, buying real estate abroad for a residence permit and citizenship will cost more than 500,000 euros.

If you are interested in selling real estate abroad, immediately discuss the purpose of the purchase. In this way, the broker will make a selection of those objects that, according to the law, will exactly meet the criteria for the preparation of documents.

Foreign real estate as a backup option for family relocation

Considering the unstable situation in the country, for Ukrainians, investments in real estate abroad have become the only tool to ensure the safety of their relatives.

In the conditions of a large influx of people abroad, the availability of housing for rent and affordable cost have become the main problems.
